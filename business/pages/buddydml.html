Buddy DML

Emmanuel Deliver Amable (born 14 April 2008), known professionally as Buddy DML, also known as The Trenchkid TM, is a Ghanaian musical artist, songwriter, singer, rapper, and record producer. His music blends hip hop, Afrobeat, R&B, melodic trap, and drill influences.

Early life

Buddy DML was born in Anloga, Volta Region, Ghana. He developed interest in music at an early age and began singing at age thirteen. His artistic identity reflects resilience, emotion, and cultural storytelling.

Career

He began recording music in 2021 and later developed a professional career under AEDTP WORLD. Between 2023 and 2024, he expanded into full music production and digital distribution.

Musical style

His sound blends Afrobeat rhythms with hip hop, drill, R&B, and melodic trap. His lyrics often focus on faith, love, struggle, ambition, and emotional growth.

Breakthrough

His breakthrough single Yansh Na Yansh gained viral attention between 2024 and 2025, establishing his presence in the Ghanaian digital music space.
