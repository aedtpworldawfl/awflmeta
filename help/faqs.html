Account & Authentication

How do I create an account?

To create an account on AWFLMETA:

  1. Click "Create Account" button in the top navigation
  2. Enter your username (letters, numbers, underscores only — no spaces)
  3. Create a password (minimum 8 characters, no spaces)
  4. Click "Create Account"
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on "Forgotten password? Reset via Email" on the Sign In page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link.

Is my account information secure?

Yes. All accounts are protected under the AEDTP WORLD FREE LICENSE (AWFL). Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact aedtpworld@gmail.com with your username and request account deletion.

Wiki Creation & Editing

What is a wiki page?

A wiki page is a document that contains metadata about a topic, person, artist, business, or concept. Pages are created using a rich text editor and published under the AWFL license.

How do I create a new wiki page?

Follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your account
  2. Click "+ Create New Wiki" or go to the Create Wiki section
  3. Enter a title (spaces become underscores in the URL)
  4. Choose a category (Artists, Music, Biography, Business, Dictionary, Education, News, Apps)
  5. Click "Create & Edit"
  6. Use the editor to format your content
  7. Save and publish your page

What categories are available?

Category URL Path Best For
🎨 Artists awfl/artists/ Artist profiles and biographies
📖 Biography awfl/biography/ Personal and professional biographical data
💼 Business awfl/business/ Company profiles and business information
📚 Dictionary awfl/dictionary/ Words, meanings, and language documentation
🎓 Education awfl/education/ Educational content and learning resources
🎵 Music awfl/music/ Music tracks, albums, and audio releases
📰 News awfl/news/ News articles and current events
📱 Apps awfl/apps/ Applications and software

What formatting options are available?

The AWFLMETA editor supports:

  • Text Formatting: Bold, Italic, Underline, Strikethrough, Superscript, Subscript
  • Headings: H1 through H6
  • Lists: Bullet points and numbered lists
  • Media: Images, Videos, Audio files, Links
  • Tables: Full table creation and formatting
  • Blocks: Quote blocks, code blocks, note boxes, warning boxes, tip boxes
  • Special Elements: Horizontal rules, Table of Contents, Infoboxes

Can I undo changes?

Yes. Use the "Undo" (↩) and "Redo" (↪) buttons in the editor toolbar, or use keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Z for undo).

How do I insert images?

To insert an image:

  1. Click the "🖼 Image" button in the toolbar
  2. Enter the image URL (use images from https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/images/)
  3. Add display text and alt text (optional)
  4. Choose alignment (full width, float left, or float right)
  5. Click "Insert"

How do I add audio files?

To insert audio:

  1. Click the "♪ Audio" button in the toolbar
  2. Enter the audio file URL (use audio from https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/audio/)
  3. Add a caption (optional)
  4. Click "Insert"

What if I have spaces in my file name?

Spaces are automatically converted to underscores (_) in URLs and filenames. For example: "My Great Artist" becomes "My_Great_Artist".

File Upload & Management

How do I upload files to AWFLMETA?

To upload files:

  1. Sign in to your account
  2. Go to the Upload section
  3. Select an HTML file from your computer (click or drag & drop)
  4. Choose destination categories (you can upload to multiple categories)
  5. Click "↑ Upload to Selected Paths"

What file types are supported?

  • Wiki Pages: .html files only
  • Images: .jpg, .jpeg, .png (stored in images/ folder)
  • Audio: .mp3, .wav, .m4a (stored in audio/ folder)

Where are my uploaded files stored?

Files are stored in category-specific folders:

  • HTML files: awfl/{category}/
  • Images: https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/images/
  • Audio: https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/audio/

Can I delete a file I uploaded?

Yes. From the editor, click the "🗑 Delete Page" button. You can only delete pages you created.

How do I export my wiki page?

Click "↓ Export HTML" in the toolbar to download your page as an HTML file.

Work Registration

What is work registration?

Work registration is the process of officially submitting your work (business, art, music, design, writing, etc.) to AEDTP WORLD for review and inclusion in the AWFLMETA database.

Who can register work?

Anyone can register work, including:

  • Developers and programmers
  • Business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Artists and musicians
  • Designers and writers
  • Freelancers and content creators

How do I register my work?

Visit the Work Registration Portal and follow these steps:

  1. Step 1 — Your Identity: Enter your full name, email, and country
  2. Step 2 — Work Details: Select your work category and provide your work/brand name
  3. Step 3 — Submit: Fill in business details (if applicable) and submit for review

What happens after I submit my registration?

AEDTP WORLD will review your registration. You'll receive an email notification about the decision. If approved, your work will be published in the appropriate category.

How long does registration review take?

Review typically takes 5-14 business days, depending on the volume of submissions.

What is a registration URL?

When you register, you get a unique URL: https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/register/{title}@{workname}.html. This is your registration file that AEDTP WORLD reviews.

Licensing & Copyright

What is the AEDTP WORLD FREE LICENSE (AWFL)?

AWFL is an open license that allows free use, distribution, and modification of content published under it. All content on AWFLMETA is published under this license.

Can I use content from AWFLMETA?

Yes. All content is openly licensed under AWFL. You can use, share, and modify it, as long as you provide proper attribution to the original author and AEDTP WORLD.

Do I need permission to republish content?

No. The AWFL license allows republication without explicit permission, but you must provide attribution.

What if I find plagiarized content?

Report it to aedtpworld@gmail.com with details. AEDTP WORLD takes copyright violations seriously.
