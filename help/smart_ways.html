Content Creation Excellence

✅ Best Practices for Wiki Pages

  • Start with a clear title: Make your wiki title descriptive and SEO-friendly. Spaces become underscores, so "Electronic_Music_Producer" is better indexed than generic titles.
  • Use structured headings: Organize content with H2 and H3 headings for better readability and table of contents generation.
  • Add infoboxes: Use the Infobox feature to display key metadata about your subject (names, dates, statistics, etc.).
  • Include multimedia: Add images from https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/images/ to make pages visually appealing.
  • Write in bite-sized paragraphs: Keep paragraphs short (3-5 sentences) for better scannability.
  • Use tables for data: Tables are perfect for comparing information, listing releases, or showing statistics.

🎯 Optimizing Your Content for Discovery

  • Keyword-rich titles: Use specific, searchable keywords. "Ghanaian_Highlife_Music" is more discoverable than "Music".
  • Comprehensive metadata: Fill in all fields completely. The more complete your profile, the higher it ranks.
  • Cross-reference related content: Link to related artists, albums, or topics within your wiki page using the 🔗 Link tool.
  • Use proper categories: Place your content in the most relevant category to improve discoverability.
  • Add descriptions: The description field in registration is crucial for search results. Write compelling 2-3 sentence summaries.

🖼️ Working with Images

  • Image URLs: Always use images from https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/images/ for consistency and reliability.
  • File naming: If your image has spaces (e.g., "Artist Portrait"), upload it as Artist_Portrait.jpg.
  • Image alignment: Use full-width for hero images, float-left for profile pictures, float-right for supporting images.
  • Alt text: Always add descriptive alt text for accessibility and SEO.
  • Image organization: Keep images organized in named subfolders within the images directory.

♪ Working with Audio

  • Audio URLs: Store audio files in https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/awflmeta/audio/.
  • File naming convention: Use underscores for spaces: Song_Name_Artist_Name.mp3
  • Supported formats: .mp3, .wav, .m4a, .ogg
  • Captions: Always add captions/descriptions for audio elements.

Account & Security

🔐 Password Security

  • Use a minimum of 8 characters (AWFLMETA requirement)
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid dictionary words and personal information
  • Use unique passwords for different accounts
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Change your password every 3-6 months

📧 Email Best Practices

  • Use a professional email address for registration
  • Keep your email address up-to-date in your profile
  • Check spam folders for AEDTP WORLD emails
  • Don't share your login credentials with others
  • Use two-factor authentication if available

Search & Discovery Optimization

🔍 SEO Best Practices

  • Keyword placement: Include main keywords in title, first paragraph, and headings
  • Meta descriptions: Write compelling descriptions that entice clicks
  • Internal links: Link to related content within AWFLMETA
  • External links: Link to authoritative sources to build credibility

  • Unique content: Original content ranks better than duplicated content

Common Mistakes to Avoid

⚠️ Avoid These Pitfalls:
  • ❌ Empty or incomplete pages — Always publish complete, substantial content
  • ❌ Broken image/audio links — Test all multimedia before publishing
  • ❌ Poor formatting — Use headings, lists, and spacing for readability
  • ❌ Plagiarized content — Always create original content or properly attribute sources
  • ❌ Inconsistent naming — Use the underscore convention throughout
  • ❌ No updates — Keep content current and relevant
  • ❌ Ignoring metadata — Complete all registration fields for better visibility

Troubleshooting

🐛 Common Issues & Solutions

Issue Solution

Advanced Features

🎨 Infobox Templates

Infoboxes are perfect for displaying structured data:
